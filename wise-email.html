Upcoming change: Wise integration migration for your team members

H
Hubstaff <no-reply@hubstaff.com>
To: Owner · Sep 8, 2025, 3:41 PM

Wise integration migration

warning
June 4 (Start date)

We're writing to give you a heads-up about an upcoming change that will affect some members (aka contractors) of your organization.

Hubstaff is migrating to a new version of its Wise integration.
The current version will be deprecated on [date], and any members who currently receive payments via Wise will need to complete a one-time migration to continue receiving payments without interruption.

Why we are doing it

The new integration brings number of benefits, including:

  • Your team members will be able to receive payments directly to their bank account — no Wise account required
  • They will no longer need to accept every transaction
  • New integration opens up payouts to the new locations

How we will notify your team members

We'll reach out to affected members through multiple channels:

  • In-app notifications in the Hubstaff desktop app and web app, starting on [date]
  • A follow-up email for any members who haven't completed the migration after approximately two weeks

The migration itself takes less than 10 minutes and only needs to be done once.

No action is required from you
Your members will be guided through the migration directly.

Tracking progress from your end

Once migration starts, you can track your team's progress on the Manage Payroll page, where each member's status is visible at a glance.

Track migration progress arrow_forward

Need help?

If you or your members have questions about the migration, the following resources are available:

  • View the FAQ
  • Contact our support team
